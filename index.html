    Frequently Asked Questions

    • Why join PGI ?

      We believe choosing PGI bharatpur will be one of the best decisions of your life because you will get to interact directly with expert mentors who will help you in preparation of licensure and further processes.

    • How to join PGI Bharatpur?

      You may sign-up through this website, call us or visit us to enroll in the classes.

    • What are the benefits of joining PGI Bharatpur?

      PGI provides flexible study time with both physical and online classes. We have our own computer lab for Computer based testing. We provide videos, notes, questions and many other facilities which are sufficient for your preparation.

    • Is it expensive to join PGI Bharatpur and take classes?

      PGI offers the most affordable package for USMLE and NCLEX preparation and processing. If you have financial constraints, you may opt for payments in installments.

    • Do I need to visit PGI Bharatpur office?

      Visiting office is a great option because you can see classes aand teachers and also can get detail information about PGI Bharatpur. However for distant students you will be able to do everything online.

    • Where is PGI Bharatpur?

      PGI Bharatpur is conveniently located on Bypass Road, Bharatpur, Chitwan. We are nearby Nahar chowk, besides Road Division office or the Ganesh Mandir. Use the google map below.
